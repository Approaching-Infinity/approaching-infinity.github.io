Most people can do the compounding math. Almost nobody can sit still long enough to let it work. A family office CIO joins us to talk about financial literacy the way allocators actually practice it: patience, process, and protecting capital across decades rather than quarters.

## In this episode

- Why financial literacy is more about behavior than formulas
- How a family office thinks about capital allocation across generations
- The habits that quietly destroy compounding, and how to design against them
- Teaching money: what actually sticks with kids and first-generation wealth builders
- Long-horizon thinking in a short-attention market
