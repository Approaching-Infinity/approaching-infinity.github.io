The transition out of uniform is its own kind of market shock. Our guest, a founder and Air Force veteran, shares what carried over from military service into entrepreneurship, what had to be unlearned, and why veterans are often better prepared for the founder journey than they think.

## In this episode

- What mission planning teaches you about operating a business under uncertainty
- The identity shift from service member to civilian to founder
- Building a network from zero after leaving the military
- Where veterans underestimate themselves, and where they overestimate the transition
- Practical first steps for veterans considering finance or entrepreneurship
