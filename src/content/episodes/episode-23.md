Tail risk gets talked about a lot and understood rarely. We sit down with an options market maker to see the market from the other side of the trade: how quotes get made, how convexity is priced, and what really happens to liquidity when stress arrives.

## In this episode

- How a market maker thinks about pricing and hedging tail scenarios
- Convexity in plain language: why small premiums can matter so much
- Market microstructure basics: spreads, depth, and who is on the other side
- Why liquidity vanishes exactly when everyone needs it
- Common retail misconceptions about buying and selling protection
