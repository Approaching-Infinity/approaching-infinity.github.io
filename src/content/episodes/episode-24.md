Everyone says AI is changing markets. Far fewer people can explain how. A quant researcher joins us to talk about what machine learning actually does inside a modern research process, and why the boring parts, data hygiene and validation, still decide who wins.

## In this episode

- Where machine learning helps a research pipeline and where it overfits
- Why data quality beats model sophistication almost every time
- Regime awareness: the quiet failure mode of trained models
- What AI tooling means for solo researchers and small teams
- Skills worth building now if you want to work in quantitative finance
