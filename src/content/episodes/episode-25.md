Volatility selling looks easy right up until it isn't. In this episode we sit down with a volatility trader to unpack how regime shifts show up in the options market before they show up in the headlines, and why the discipline to step aside is a skill in itself.

## In this episode

- How implied and realized volatility diverge at regime turning points
- Reading the volatility surface: skew, term structure, and what they signal
- Why carry trades feel safest right before they are most dangerous
- Position sizing and exits when the environment stops cooperating
- Building a process that survives more than one market regime
