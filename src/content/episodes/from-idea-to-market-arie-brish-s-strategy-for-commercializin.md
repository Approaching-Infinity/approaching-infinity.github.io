In this episode of Approaching Infinity, we are honored to host Arie Brish, a renowned expert in the realm of business innovation and strategy. Arie delves into the critical importance of execution in innovation, sharing insights drawn from both historical successes and his vast experience. He sheds light on the distinction between technology and true innovation, emphasizing the necessity of understanding customer needs to achieve success. Arie's journey from conceptualizing groundbreaking ideas to commercializing them offers a wealth of knowledge.
