In this episode of Approaching Infinity, join me, a seasoned engineer and finance professional with over eight years of cross-sector experience, as I discuss my journey and passion for innovation, technology, and finance. Get insights into my various roles, from co-founding Limitless Capital, a boutique hedge fund specializing in a long/short portfolio of U.S equity options, to data analysis at Barbaricum, capital markets analysis at Drexel Hamilton, and program management in the United States Air Force, among others!
